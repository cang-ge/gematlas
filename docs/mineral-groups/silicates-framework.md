# Silicates — Framework (Tectosilicates)

## Overview

Framework silicates have a SiO₂ / Al₂O₃ molar ratio around
3:1–6:1. The most important gem-bearing family is the feldspar
group (moonstone, labradorite, sunstone, amazonite); zeolites
are occasionally cut. Feldspars account for ~60% of Earth's
crustal mineral volume.


## Formula Class

M(Al,Si)₄O₈

## Crystal Structure

[SiO₄]⁴⁻ tetrahedra share all four oxygens, forming a 3D
infinite framework. In feldspars, Si⁴⁺ / Al³⁺ substitution
occurs at tetrahedral sites; alkali / alkaline-earth cations
fill structural cavities. The Si/Al ratio and cation type
determine the species.
